## Why n8n?

In a world full of automation tools, n8n stands out because it's:

- **Open Source**: Full control over your data and workflows
- **Self-Hostable**: Keep sensitive data on your own infrastructure
- **Visual**: Drag-and-drop interface that makes complex workflows understandable
- **Extensible**: Custom nodes and JavaScript support for unlimited possibilities
- **Cost-Effective**: No per-operation pricing - run as much as you need
